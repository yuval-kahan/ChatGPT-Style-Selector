Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2024

At Right Click Assistant, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

Right Click Assistant does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We believe in complete transparency and minimal data handling:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not store any user data on our servers
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Local Storage

The extension only uses local browser storage to save your preferences:

  • Custom search engine configurations
  • Extension settings and preferences
  • All data is stored locally on your device

Required Permissions

Our extension requires minimal permissions to function:

  • contextMenus: To create and manage right-click menu items
  • storage: To save your settings locally
  • activeTab: To access the current tab's URL and selected text

Data Security

We prioritize your data security through:

  • Local-only data processing
  • No external data transmission
  • Open-source code for transparency

Third-Party Services

When you use search features, you will be redirected to your chosen search engine (e.g., ChatGPT, Google, X). Please note that:

  • These third-party services have their own privacy policies
  • We recommend reviewing their privacy policies
  • We are not responsible for their data handling practices

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:
